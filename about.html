About us...

Located in the heart of Copenhagen, the atmosphere of the board game cafe immerses you in a moment of conviviality, relaxation, and fun with friends and family.
A multitude of board games as much as you want, provided that you buy drinks or food on the site. Playable alone up to an infinity of players, discover an ultra complete selection of board games with various themes and types: retro/classic games, quiz games, management games, dexterity games, bluffing games, investigation games, or strategy games…
The idea for this board game cafe came straight from Canada, during a trip. We fell in love with this idea which combines the passion for the game and sharing a special experience.
Our principle, in a hygge spirit, is to connect through play.
So don't wait and book your table and follow us on social media to not miss our events.
